At Night

Typeface

10 / 2012

Role: Creator

Working late one night, I looked out of a window that was segmented into quadrants. I wondered if every letter could be created from those squares alone. An additional diagonal subdivision was required, but I'm quite happy with the result. Released under the SIL Open Font License.
