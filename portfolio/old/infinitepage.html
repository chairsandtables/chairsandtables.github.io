Though a very frustrating experience in this demo, looping scrollbars can be used effectively for content with no strong order. In a page with three distinct sections, for example, infinite scroll ensures that all content is at most one section away.

I wrote this demo in pure JavaScript, so no libraries are needed. It should work all the way back to Netscape, though I've yet to try it on mobile browsers.
